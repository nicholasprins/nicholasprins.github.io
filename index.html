Hi, I'm Nicholas Prinsloo

Web developer & Junior Programmer in training

I am a creative, talented and driven individual with a versatile skill set. My journey in coding started in 2017, as HTML was part of my high school curriculum for Computer Applications Technology and since then I have attended 2 coding boot camps. The first was sponsored by Standard Bank in June 2018 and was primarily focused the Python language, that is where I got my taste for coding! We worked with Python 3 and HTML5 on Raspbian Linux.

At this point in time, I was still starting out with HTML and wasn’t expecting to do spectacularly well with the coding challenges presented, but to our surprise my team excelled and emerged victorious by the end of the challenge as the overall winners! The second boot camp I had attended was at a coding school called WeThinkCode_ in January 2020 and its curriculum is based on that of 42 Paris, which is a private, non-profit and tuition-free computer programming school. This boot camp was an introduction to programming in C and using the Git system. Aside from this boot camp being as intensive as it was, I did enjoy it to the fullest extent. I would even go so far as to say that that experience turned my "flame" into a raging fire.
